# fdma_go_jp

Data from the Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications Fire and Disaster Management Agency on emergency transport difficulties during the COVID-19 pandemic.

## Data

- [Japan-wide CSV](https://code4fukui.github.io/fdma_go_jp/emergencytransport_difficult_all.csv)
- [Prefecture-specific CSV](https://code4fukui.github.io/fdma_go_jp/emergencytransport_difficult_pref.csv)
- [Fire department-specific CSV](https://code4fukui.github.io/fdma_go_jp/emergencytransport_difficult.csv)

The "emergency transport difficulty cases" refer to cases where the emergency squad had to make 4 or more hospital referrals and the scene time was 30 minutes or more, as reported by each fire department to the Fire and Disaster Management Agency.
